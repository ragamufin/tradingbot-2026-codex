* Better System Trader 062: PJ Sutherland
  * ROC
  * volatility measure -> ma of ma of ATR/close, rising means vol is rising
  * quantifying fear and greed in order to sell greed and buy fear
  * can use leverage
  * larger cap assets support mr more
  * don't wait for confirmation, catch falling knives
  * likes to look for steep intra-day sell-offs
  * diversify across strategies/assets by looking at average number of trades and average length of trades. You can use this to adjust parameters for each strategy to get them to be close to each other based on the two metrics
  * For risk management
    * time stops
    * profit stop
    * don't use stop losses, instead position size based on the average worst-case drawdowns historically
  * On the short side be tighter on parameters such as entries and exits
    * often you see sharp down spikes that revert quickly
